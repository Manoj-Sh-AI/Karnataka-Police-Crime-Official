About Us

The Karnataka Police is responsible for a wide range of activities and functions, including crime prevention, law enforcement, traffic management, and emergency response. It is divided into several units and divisions, each with its own specific responsibilities and areas of focus. The Karnataka Police works closely with other law enforcement agencies and with the community to ensure the safety and security of the people of Karnataka.

MURDER MAP VISUALIZATION
OF KARNATAKA

In India, the information stored in criminal records stored in police databases can be accessed in the form of excel sheets but our goal is to retrieve the data from the police database and display it on a website in the form of location in the map, pie charts, and other crime information in a single website. Later this website can be integrated into mobile applications also. This will be easy for police officers to complete their crime visits easily and informatively.
